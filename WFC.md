# 💻 The Human Cognome Project (HCP) 

Welcome to the build. We’re mapping 1.4 million "tokens" (units of thought) into a 3D digital brain. It’s like Google Maps, but for ideas instead of streets.

### 🛡️ The PORTGNOME Layer
Most AI needs a $2,000 PC to "think." We think that sucks. 
**PORTGNOME** is our custom C++ hardware guardian. It benchmarks your device's RAM and CPU on the fly:
* **High Spec:** It gobbles RAM for max speed.
* **Potato Spec:** It uses `mmap` to run straight off the disk so your phone doesn't melt.

#### 3. PBM Mapping (The "Architects")
We are building **Pair-Bond Maps**. This is Phase 2.

* **Skill:** Math, Logic, Linguistics.
